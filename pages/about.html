Julius Juan Justianto (b. 1997 Ujung Pandang, Indonesia) is a photographer based in Jakarta –who specializes in fashion as his subject matter. Years of experience working across discplines for esteemed clientele, and with individuals from various sectors of the industry, –lead him to develop a very particular voice and perspective that distinguishes him from the crowd.


Consistently experimenting with beauty, mystery and a touch of absurdity through his lens, his visual story-telling aims to capture the balance of witted theatricalities and raw deep-rooted honesty.
